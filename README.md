# EMIL POST

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **LOGIKĒ**.

EMIL POST (1897–1954) — LOGIKĒ, the great ALMOST-FIRST. Working alone in New York in the 1920s he reached the core of [[kurt-godel]]'s incompleteness and [[alan-turing]]'s undecidability — BEFORE either — but illness & a cautious editor kept it unpublished, and the credit went elsewhere. ⚑ PRODUCTION SYSTEMS: computation is just REWRITING STRINGS BY RULES — a model as powerful as the Turing machine, the ancestor of every formal grammar & term-rewriting system. ⚑ THE POST CORRESPONDENCE PROBLEM: given dominoes each with a top & bottom string, can you lay some in a row so the top reads the same as the bottom? Sounds like a game; it's UNDECIDABLE — no algorithm solves it in general, so it's the standard tool for proving OTHER problems undecidable. LIVE: play PCP — stack dominoes so top=bottom; a bounded solver finds short matches (verified a known solvable instance); deciding solvability in general is impossible. CONFIDENCE-TIERED: STRONG (production/rewriting systems are a complete model of computation; PCP is rigorously undecidable & the everyday undecidability tool) / THE-PRIORITY-HE-LOST (⚠ anticipated Gödel c.1921 & the Turing-machine idea but didn't publish in time; the anticipation is now acknowledged, the formal credit fairly went to those who published) / A-LIFE-OF-OBSTACLES (lost an arm at 13, severe bipolar disorder, heavy teaching load, died at 57). ⚑ [[alan-turing]]'s halting problem wearing a child's puzzle; the man who saw the limits first.
